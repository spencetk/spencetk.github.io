About Me

Hello all, my name is Spencer Keefer and I'm currently a junior at Virginia Tech majoring in Computer Science. Outside of my courses I'm a member of the Hybrid Electric Vehicle Team (HEVT) and Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity here at Virginia Tech. After taking my first programming course in Java my freshmen year, I knew I wanted to pursue a degree in Computer Science! Withi this website I hope to document and share my interests with the public about who I am and what drives me!
